About

Jump Continue is site for drafting Jumpstart packs, by providing a simple and easy to use interface for randomly distributing or choosing from a selection of themes.

Major functions:

  • Configuration of available packs, saved in the browser or by copying a code
  • Display of key cards in different theme variants
  • Three modes of of distribution:
    • Random pair (2 themes per player, no fresh themes for second pick)
    • Random pool, pick two (3+ themes per player, no fresh themes for second pick)
    • Two random pools, pick a theme from each (with fresh themes for second pick enabled)
  • Export decklist for MTGA
